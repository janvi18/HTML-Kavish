    Gujarati

    Gujarati food is a style of cooking native to the Indian state of Gujarat. It is characterized by the use of whole spices, fresh garlic, ginger, and chilies, and a variety of vegetables, lentils, and grains. Gujarati cuisine is also known for its use of peanuts, sesame seeds, and coconut.
